INDIA GATE

India Gate: Commemorates Indian soldiers of World War I. Inscribed with their names. Eternal flame, Amar Jawan Jyoti, honors fallen heroes. Central canopy. Symbol of sacrifice and national pride.

JANTAR MANTAR

Jantar Mantar: Astronomical instruments dating to 1724. Observe the sundial's accuracy. Ascertain celestial movements with the Samrat Yantra. Intriguing geometric structures. Rich historical and scientific significance.

HUMAYUN TOMB

Humayun's Tomb: Mughal architecture masterpiece. Red sandstone, white marble elegance. Char Bagh garden symmetry. Intricate lattice work. Tomb of Emperor Humayun. UNESCO World Heritage Site. Majestic and serene ambiance.
